Design For x, also commonly know as DFx, is a design method commonly used in product development. One of the great benifits of DFx is that x can be whatever you need it to be. It is a method that can be used to refine your design to apply to certain criteria, but also the results of DFx methods can be combined to improve the your final concept.

Design for Environment is a thing that people like to use when they don't want to aid the destruction of our already dying planet!

Design for Manufacture is used to make stuff as easy to make as possible!

Design for Cost is for when your client runs out of money!
